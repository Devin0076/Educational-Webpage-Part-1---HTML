The Super Smash Bros. games

What is Super Smash Bros.?

Super Smash Bros. is a crossover platform fighting game series developed by Nintendo.

The critically acclaimed game series made its debut in 1999 when the first game released on the Nintendo 64

What does it feature?

  • Characters from various Nintendo franchises and third party franchises
  • Platform-based fighting mechanics
  • Multiplayer modes
  • Iconic stages and items from different games

What are the games in the series?

  1. Super Smash Bros. (1999) (N64)
  2. Super Smash Bros. Melee (2001) (Gamecube)
  3. Super Smash Bros. Brawl (2008) (Wii)
  4. Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U (2014)
  5. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (2018) (Nintendo Switch)
